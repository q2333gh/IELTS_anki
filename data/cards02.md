## The company offered a bonus as an **incentive** to boost employee performance.

## The police investigated the **incident** at the scene.

**incident** 是一个事件或发生的事情，通常是异常或值得注意的。

## The park was **inaccessible** due to the snowstorm.

## His monthly **income** has increased this year.
